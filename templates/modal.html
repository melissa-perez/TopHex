TopHex

TopHex is a tool that allows you to check the color content of an image. To use the program on an image:

  • securely upload an image from you local computer
  • hit the random bottom to generate an image from Unsplash.com/sky

After an image is imported into the interface, the top five hex colors will be shown under two categories: complete and simplified.

Complete palette
Uses the original and complete range of hex colors in an image. May result in similar colors.
Simplified palette
Uses a simplified palette from the original. May result in diverse colors with possible information loss.

Only .jpg/.jpeg/.png image formats are supported.
